All about me…

I’m a Graphic Designer with over twenty years experience. I have worked mainly in corporate design environments but have also worked in the education and public sectors, in advertising and in publications.

In my spare time I love to hang out with family and friends and have a strong appreciation for the talents of artisans in the food and wine industry.

Most recently I worked in the Mercer Design Studio managing forms for Mercer Australia Pacific and assisting the studio with overflow design work.

Just prior to this I worked as a freelance graphic designer for William Angliss Institute, VicRoads and the Victorian Department of Transport, Public Affairs Division.
